1. to take something as given; to assume
	1. `imomotel kol alamah, xo kahuehzah ttarain`
	   assuming it will rain, I bring an umbrella
2. to suppose; to posit mentally
	1. `akata uo kiippozkatel kol alamah, xo moh ihahuaun`
	   supposing that we find the treasure, I will celebrate
3. to treat or regard as
	1. `Daniel ouccou sihatel kol alamahn`
	   I regard Daniel as my leader
4. to grant for the sake of argument
